1. Who we are

Updated is a private team accountability app operated by Alejandro Martinez. If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_SUPPORT_EMAIL.

2. Information we collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Name and email address
  • Account role and approval status
  • Contribution goals
  • Check-in and check-out session data
  • Contribution notes entered by users
  • Support messages you send us

3. How we use information

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To store contribution activity and team reporting data
  • To show team dashboards, history, and reports
  • To approve or manage team accounts
  • To respond to support requests
  • To maintain app security and reliability

4. Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may use service providers that help operate the app, including cloud infrastructure and authentication providers needed to run the product.

5. Data retention

We keep account and contribution data for as long as needed to provide the app, unless a user deletes their account or we are required to retain information for legal or security reasons.

6. Account deletion

Users can initiate account deletion inside the app in Settings by selecting Delete My Account. When account deletion is completed, account records and associated contribution data are permanently removed from active systems, subject to any legally required retention.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. Children’s privacy

Updated is not intended for children under 13.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date on this page.
