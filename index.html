Frequently Asked Questions

I created an account but cannot check in.

Your account may still be waiting for admin approval. Once an admin approves your account, you can log in and use check-in and reporting features.

I forgot my password.

If password reset is enabled in the app’s backend, use the available reset flow. If you need help, contact support at the email above.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to Settings, then choose Delete My Account. This permanently deletes your profile, login, and contribution data.

What data does Updated store?

Updated stores account information, team approval status, goals, and contribution session data needed for check-ins, reports, and team visibility.

Support Scope

Support is available for sign-in issues, account approval issues, contribution history questions, account deletion questions, and general product feedback.
